    Time to party! The Captcha Design Festival
    is taking place for the tenth time this year!

    The festival is organized by a group of students from the University of Applied Sciences Mannheim each fall. Students from all over the world can register for the festival and attend our workshops the following days in order to broaden their horizons and gain a new perspective on design. For a week onward after the festival has finished, starting on Saturday, the 10th of September, the results will be exhibited at Kunstverein Mannheim.
